About me .

Two years ago, I quit my job of 8 years to pursue a career. Why Web Design? I have a natural curiosity for how things work. I thrive on making and creating. In addition, I found that my borderline OCD for organization and attention to details has dubbed me Web Designer material. As an avid Internet user, I appreciate a clean and user-friendly Website design, and I hold minimal tolerance for fluff. With an inherent desire to succeed, I excel at execution and problem-solving. Give me a concept and I will deliver.
