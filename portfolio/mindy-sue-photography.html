Portfolio .

This is a Website redesign (including the logo) that I completed for a friend, who was in desparate need of an update. Being a photography site, I built it in on Wordpress to allow her to blog and add photos to the portfolio. I learned a lot about the ins and outs of Wordpress themes on this project, because I had to make many customizations to the template files in order to achieve the patterns and styles I wanted.

This project is still a work in progress. While she gets her initial blogs together, I am adding the finishing touches before we go live with promoting publicly. Additonally, I will add analytics and explore ways to optimize the site speed.
