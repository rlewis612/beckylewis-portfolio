Portfolio .

This is a business Website that I designed and created for a client. It is a locally-owned construction company in Amarillo, TX that services Southern and Midwest states. The initial goal of this Website was to create an online presence and establish credibility, starting with Amarillo. Roofers can sometimes have a tainted reputation that elicits skepticism due to the nature of the industry. As a result, I wanted to create a straight-forward, welcoming, and calming Website that makes the user feel at ease.

From concept to deliverables (sketch, wireframes, mockups), I designed and built this Website from raw code/markup using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. This is still a work in progress, as I have plans to include a blog page to help with credibility on the business side, and SEO on the marketing side. Additionally, I implemented an adwords campaign for the business owner and will be overseeing that to incorporate with the Website SEO strategies.
